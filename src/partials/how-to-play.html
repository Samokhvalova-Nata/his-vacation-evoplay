How to play

  • Match elements: Combine similar items to meet the level's objectives.

  • Unlock stories: Progress through the game to unlock heartwarming stories about the pets you help.

  • Use boosters: Earn and use powerful boosters to clear obstacles and complete levels more efficiently.

  • Collect rewards: Earn rewards and bonuses through your puzzle-solving prowess to enhance your gameplay experience.

  • Complete missions: Each level has specific goals to achieve, helping different pets in need.
